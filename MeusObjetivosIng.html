MY GOALS

This humble text aims to quickly demonstrate my deepest ambitions that carry me day after day.
I have always been a big dreamer who has never been able to give up on my goals. To this day I still remember the deepest dreams I had as a child. Of course, nowadays I am a much more realistic person than when I was a child and so, without further ado I will mention my main dreams.
1- Being rich: Of course, the main dream of someone like me is to be rich, that is, to try hard enough to be worthy of a great salary
2- Participate in something big: I always wanted to be part of something big that many people could enjoy, even if I don't receive as much recognition as the other members.
3- Helping a lot of people: last but not least, I always liked helping a lot of people, regardless of whether they know who is helping them.
