MY DREAMS

This short text aims to show some of the impossible things that I still want to achieve.
Here I'm going to talk about unrealistic goals in my view, but it doesn't cost anything to try again and again until I achieve them. My main dreams are:
1- Become a global Valorant player, an online game in which two teams face each other to achieve an objective.
2- Becoming a famous singer, that is, being able to simply go up on a stage and be able to release my voice.
3- Become a great martial arts fighter, because this way I will have knowledge of different techniques that will allow me to have a noble battle with an opponent.
